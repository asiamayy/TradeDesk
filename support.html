Frequently asked
How does the free trial work?
Every new account starts with a 7-day free trial of TradeDesk Pro. You'll need to enter a card to start, but you won't be charged until day 8. Cancel anytime during the trial and you won't be billed.
How do I cancel my subscription?
You can cancel anytime from your Stripe billing portal, or by emailing info@jointradedesk.com and we'll cancel it for you. See the Refunds & Cancellation tab for full details.
Is the pricing in a proposal accurate?
Proposals are generated using AI based on the rates and details you provide. Always review pricing, scope, and terms before sending a proposal to a client — TradeDesk is a drafting tool, not a substitute for your own judgment.
Can I use TradeDesk for trades other than roofing?
TradeDesk is currently built and tuned specifically for roofing contractors. If you'd like to see it expand to other trades, let us know — we're listening.
Is my data secure?
Yes. Passwords are securely hashed and never stored in plain text, and all traffic is encrypted. See our Privacy Policy for full details.

This page explains how billing, cancellation, and refunds work for TradeDesk subscriptions.

Free trial

New accounts include a 7-day free trial of TradeDesk Pro. A payment method is required to start the trial, but you will not be charged during this period.

If you cancel before the trial ends, you will not be billed anything. If you take no action, your subscription automatically converts to a paid plan at $29/month starting on day 8.

Billing

After your trial, TradeDesk is billed at $29/month and renews automatically each billing cycle until canceled. Payments are processed securely through Stripe.

How to cancel

You can cancel your subscription at any time, for any reason, in one of two ways:

  1. Email info@jointradedesk.com from your account email and request cancellation — we'll process it promptly and confirm by reply.
  2. Manage your subscription directly through the billing portal linked in your Stripe receipt emails.

When you cancel, you'll continue to have access through the end of your current billing period (or trial period). You will not be charged again after that.

Refunds

Because the free trial gives you a full 7 days to evaluate TradeDesk before any charge occurs, completed monthly payments are generally non-refundable.

That said, if you believe you were charged in error, were billed after a cancellation request, or have an unusual circumstance, email info@jointradedesk.com and we'll look into it. Refund requests are reviewed on a case-by-case basis.
