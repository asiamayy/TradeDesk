This Privacy Policy explains what information TradeDesk collects, how it's used, and how it's protected.

Information We Collect

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Account information: your email address and password (stored as a securely hashed value, never in plain text).
  • Business information: your company name, your name, phone number, license number, and pricing rates — used to generate your proposals.
  • Job and client details you enter: client names, addresses, and job descriptions you provide when generating a proposal.
  • Payment information: handled directly by Stripe, our payment processor. TradeDesk receives confirmation of your subscription status but does not store your card number or banking details.
How We Use Your Information
  • To operate your account and generate proposals on your behalf.
  • To process subscription payments and manage billing through Stripe.
  • To communicate with you about your account or the Service.
  • To maintain and improve the Service.

Job details you enter (client names, addresses, job notes) are sent to Anthropic's Claude API to generate your proposal content. This data is used solely to produce your proposal and is not used to train AI models.

Data Storage & Security

Your account data is stored in a secure database (Cloudflare D1). Passwords are hashed using industry-standard methods (PBKDF2) and are never stored or transmitted in plain text. All communication between your device and our servers is encrypted (HTTPS).

Third-Party Services

TradeDesk relies on the following third-party services to operate:

  • Anthropic (Claude API) — generates proposal content from your job descriptions.
  • Stripe — processes subscription payments.
  • Cloudflare — hosts our backend infrastructure and database.
  • GitHub Pages — hosts the TradeDesk web application.

Each of these providers has its own privacy practices governing the data they process on our behalf.
